GhostDetect uses AI to scan your payroll in seconds, flag fraudulent records with confidence scores, and block disbursements through Squad API until identity is verified.
AI anomaly detection
Random Forest model trained on 5,000 Nigerian payroll records. Flags NIN duplicates, salary outliers, and missing fields instantly.
Squad payment gating
Verified workers get paid. Flagged records are blocked. The money cannot move until AI confirms the identity is legitimate.
Full audit trail
Every decision logged — who flagged it, the AI reasons, timestamp, and Squad reference. Complete accountability for every kobo.
